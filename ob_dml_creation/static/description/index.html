DML creation product extend

Related to product for DML

This module add product group and product sequence on product level and also allow to filter on bases of internal category and product sequence.

Select product internal category

You can select product internal category as per your require.

Create product sequence

You can create product sequence from Sale->Configuration->Product Category & Attribute -> Product sequence

List view product sequence

You can edit or delete product sequence from list view of product sequence from Sale->Configuration->Product Category & Attribute -> Product sequence

Select product sequence in product view.

You can select product sequence which you created or also you can create from product view.

Filter product in sale order

Once product internal category and group & sequence are added, it filter the selection on Order line items.

Example

Example : Product sequence field on sales order line when adding new product on sales order. Based on the given sequence product list to be filtered while drop down opens up.
